Isopod Girls

I provided character designs and comics for a (semi-)educational project called Isopod Girls! Commercial entomologist Roach Crossing has used these characters to help expand his audience, and to provide encouragement to fellow bug-keepers.

For this project, I was tasked with designing flirty, anime-inspired characters who resemble different breeds of isopods. To strengthen the "Isopod Girls" brand, I made sure the girls' personalities and fashion senses were distinct and easy to read.
